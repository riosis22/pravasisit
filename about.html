About Us

Pravasis IT Solutions Pvt Ltd is a registered company owned by the Kerala State Pravasi Welfare Development Co-operative Society Limited No: 4455. Operating since 2008, the firm specializes in software design and development for various sectors, primarily financial entities. With over 500 clients statewide, Pravasis has established itself as a reliable IT partner. Over the years, the company has been recognized for its innovative approach and commitment to delivering high-quality, tailored IT solutions. By leveraging advanced technology and deep industry expertise, Pravasis consistently helps clients achieve operational excellence and drive growth in their respective fields.

  • Leading the way in financial software with a proven track record of over 500 satisfied clients across the state.
  • Pioneering projects tailored to cooperative banks, societies, Kudumbasree units, and other financial institutions
  • Commitment to innovation and client-centric solutions since 2016
  • Recognized for providing unparalleled customer support and ensuring long-term partnerships through reliable and scalable IT services.

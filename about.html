About Us

Founded in 2008, Pravasis IT Solutions Pvt Ltd has emerged as a trusted leader in software development, catering to the unique needs of the financial sector. Proudly owned by the Kerala State Pravasi Welfare Development Co-operative Society Limited (Reg. No: 4455), our company is committed to delivering innovative, reliable, and efficient IT solutions.

  • Leading the way in financial software with a proven track record of over 500 satisfied clients across the state.
  • Pioneering projects tailored to cooperative banks, societies, Kudumbasree units, and other financial institutions
  • Commitment to innovation and client-centric solutions since 2016

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What industries does Pravasis IT Solutions cater to?

Pravasis IT Solutions specializes in developing software for financial entities, including cooperative societies, Nidhi companies, Chitty companies, and other financial institutions.

What are the key features of Sahakari MIS?

Sahakari MIS offers comprehensive accounting tools, cloud accessibility, and customization for diverse financial institutions like cooperative societies and inventory companies.

Can Easy Collect be integrated with existing banking systems?

Yes, Easy Collect is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing systems, providing real-time updates and efficient collection management for cooperative banks.

How does Muttathe Mulla benefit Kudumbasree units?

Muttathe Mulla streamlines loan tracking and repayment processes, offering data visualization and integration with financial systems to improve operational efficiency for Kudumbasree units.

What is the support policy for Pravasis IT Solutions' software products?

Pravasis IT Solutions offers comprehensive support for all its products, including troubleshooting, regular updates, and customization assistance. Our dedicated support team ensures timely resolutions to client queries and provides ongoing maintenance to keep the systems running smoothly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Muttathe Mulla used for?

Muttathe Mulla is a mobile app designed to help Kudumbasree units track loan disbursements, manage repayments, and ensure smooth financial operations.

Is Muttathe Mulla easy to use for non-technical users?

Yes, the app features a simple, user-friendly interface that makes it accessible even for users with minimal technical knowledge.

Does Muttathe Mulla support multiple users or roles?

Yes, the app allows role-based access, ensuring administrators, field staff, and other users can securely access the information they need.

Can Muttathe Mulla be integrated with existing financial systems?

Absolutely, the app seamlessly integrates with existing financial systems to synchronize data and streamline operations.

Does Muttathe Mulla work offline?

Yes, the app supports offline functionality, letting users record loan details and repayments without an internet connection. Data syncs automatically when back online.

Features

Features

Cloud-Based Accounting Excellence



  • Sahakari MIS is a comprehensive cloud-based accounting solution designed for Nidhi companies, societies, KMLs, chitty companies, and inventory-based businesses.
  • The software includes a mobile application that allows collection agents to manage daily collections efficiently.
  • Separate user logins enable tracking of transactions, preventing fraud and enhancing security.
  • MIS passbooks are provided to clients, allowing them to monitor their financial accounts and activities in real-time.
  • Auditors can securely log in to a dedicated report section for seamless report generation and review.
  • An integrated SMS tracker helps organizations monitor SMS usage and maintain effective communication with customers.

Seamless Collection Management



  • RIO Easy Collect replaces traditional collection devices with a mobile application, enabling agents to handle daily collections paperlessly.
  • Customers receive instant SMS notifications to acknowledge receipt of their payments, ensuring transparency.
  • A separate mobile application for bank secretaries provides complete oversight of agents activities and prevents fraudulent practices./span>
  • Customers can track their financial transactions in real-time using the MIS passbook feature integrated into the system.

Innovative Loan Tracking



  • The Mulla Mithra mobile application helps Kudumbasree units manage loans disbursed under the government scheme "Muttathe Mulla."
  • Users can accurately track loan repayments, ensuring accountability and efficiency in the process.
  • Detailed reports can be generated to monitor loan disbursement and repayment trends, aiding decision-making..
  • The user-friendly design of the application makes it easy for Kudumbasree members to adopt and use the system effectively..
